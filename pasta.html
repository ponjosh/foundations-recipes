Yummy Pasta Dish

Yummy pasta dish is a classic American Italian dish

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pasta sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Shallots

Instructions

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add Pasta to boiling water, boil until soft
  3. Strain Pasta and run cold water over it
  4. Cook Ground Beef, Garlic, and Shallots together
  5. Mix the Pasta, Meat, and Pasta Sauce together
